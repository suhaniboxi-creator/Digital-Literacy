Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

Do's

* Use a professional profile name and photo
* Respect others and communicate politely
* Be mindful of your digital footprint
* Keep your privacy settings secure
* Verify information before sharing

Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information
* Do not spread fake news or rumors
* Avoid using abusive language online
* Don’t use fake profiles or impersonate others
* Don’t post when you are angry or emotional and avoid participating in online arguments or toxic debates
