Stay Safe Online Checklist

* Always check website URLs 
* Do not share personal information like OTP, passwords, or ID details
* Keep your apps and devices updated with latest security patches
* Be careful while using public Wi-Fi, especially for important logins
* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
* Entering UPI PIN means money is being sent, not received
* Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers
* Always double-check payment details before confirming any transaction
* Report immediately on National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
